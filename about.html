About Scot Gillespie

Journey in Photography

Scot Gillespie is a dedicated photographer passionate about capturing authentic moments and creating engaging visual content. His photography journey has explored various styles and techniques, with a strong focus on the organic imagery of flowers and plant life.

He documents technical work on his blog that covers shot composition and demonstrates how unique lenses can create surreal imagery. He often uses adapted projector lenses to produce distinctive blur and creative compositions.
